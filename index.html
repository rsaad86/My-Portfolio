About Me

Hello and welcome to my Portfolio website. Here you will find a little bit about me and the work I have done, which you can find below.

I am 35 years old, but who's counting really. I was born in Kuwait, and moved to Canada at the age of 4. I lived in Scarborough most of my young life, before moving to the state of Maryland for two years. I returned to Mississauga around high school. I went to York University for Information Technology, and then followed my passion and took a 9 month course in Sound and Music Recording at the Recording Arts of Canada in Toronto. I then got a diploma in Accounting at Sheridan College. However, for many years, I have worked as a field technician, from providing maintenance services for bank machines to most recently fixing computers for Dell. Now I am learning how to code to expand my knowledge and go down a new path in life.
